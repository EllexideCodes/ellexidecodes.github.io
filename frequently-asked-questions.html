Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is the price of your profile layouts?
    • I don't have a price, ma cherie. I ask for my commissioner's budget range and work within those bounds. It is so that users are not completely emptying their pockets. I don't like the thought of people having a slightly heavy heart in giving their payment for the layouts I offer.

  2. Will you put in the same amount of effort for every profile?
    • Unfortunately, no. Making layouts take time, effort, and pretty much brain power to come up with a relatively unique design. As much as I love coding, business is also business. I will, however, make it as beautiful as I am able within the bounds of our agreement.

  3. Do you still make free codes?
    • I do. I have 3 different kinds of free codes available:
      • Premade Codes -- These are codes that were previously requested from random users that are fashioned after their character and pushed to be public.
      • Gifted Codes -- These are codes that are specifically fashioned after your character and aesthetic. Depending on personal relationship and/or previous agreement, these codes may or may not be pushed to be a premade code.
      • Raffle Codes -- These are layouts that somebody wins by joining and winning a profile raffle. These are not pushed to become public.

  4. What do I need to commission a code?
    • The most basic thing you need are picture/s and budget range. Though I may ask multiple question during the Information Exchange, those two are the most important and is what I'll be asking first. Everything after that will just be consistent communication via Discord or e-mail.

  5. How long does it usually take for you to finish your code?
    • It always varies depending on my schedule, so unfortunately, I can't give you a concrete answer for this one.

  6. What is the price of your other services? Photo rendering, profile add ons, etc.
    • They most certainly vary as well. But they are usually around the $2 - $5 dollar range. Note: The higher the complexity, the larger the fee.
  7. Do you think I missed a question? Feel free to tell me.
