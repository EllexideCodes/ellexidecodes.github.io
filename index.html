Introduction & Overview

Hello! Welcome to the official website of Ellexide Codes. This website takes you through all information about commissioning, requesting, or even simply viewing E.C. Layouts and/or E.C. Products.

Ellexide Codes does not only offer layouts but also basic photoediting, profile add ons, JavaScript flairs, intermediate coding lessons and layouts for studying.

Elle offers help to those aiming to be profile makers themselves, though she does not claim to know everything. There is still much to be improved but if she can somehow help, she will.

If the layouts showcased in this website is not up to your fancy, E.C. also offers links to other Profile Makers that she admires and would highly recommend based off of their work.

Disclaimer: Because of the time limitation of juggling her layout making, life, job, and other different responsibilities, she is not guaranteed to give help.

Note: All E.C. Products does not have to be for an E.C. Layout. You may have a devilslocketdesign and have a E.C. profile add on and that is perfectly fine. All products offered are non-exclusive and profile/user friendly.
